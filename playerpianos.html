Player Pianos



Piano Disc and QRS systems are revolutionary systems to turn any piano into a player piano. Enjoy professional piano music, with or without background accompaniment, any time. This is a great feature for your home, whether you entertain, or merely enjoy live music. These systems have a large number of available music and options. They also allow for a pianist to play an acoustical piano with headphones so they do not disturb others. They also allow for the recording of music.
